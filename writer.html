Erin E. Bond

children's writer

Hello! I'm an early-career children's writer in Seattle, WA. Currently, I am revising picture book manuscripts and drafting a middle grade novel. As a former teacher, I'm passionate about stories with clever language play and strong, unlikely friendships.

I'm a graduate of Jolie Stekly's Writing for Children series and a member of SCBWI. Come say "hi" at the summer conference - you'll recognize me because, decades after my typewriter days, I still make this face while I think.

When I'm not writing, revising or squirreling away craft tips, you can find me building websites at Code.org. Away from the keyboard, I enjoy exploring beaches with my marine biologist husband, learning how to ski (ok, mostly drinking hot chocolate) and backpacking with our dog, Morty.
