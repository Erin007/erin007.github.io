I'm a full-stack software development student at Ada Developers Academy in Seattle, WA. With a background in neuroscience and education, I was previously an informal science educator, elementary school teacher and private learning specialist.

When I'm not coding, you can find me perusing the children's/YA sections of local bookstores, exploring beaches with marine biologist husband, learning how to ski (okay, mostly drinking hot chocolate) or backpacking with our dog, Morty.

Considering a collaboration? I'm always interested in technology projects with a social mission. Particular interests include EdTech innovation and curriculum development - especially causes that support literacy, girls in STEM and students with learning differences.
